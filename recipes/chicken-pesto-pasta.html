Chicken Pesto Pasta

Credit: Buzzfeed.com/Tasty.com

Description

A pesto pasta recipe using store bought pasta and pesto sauce

Ingredients

  • Pack of Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • sun-dried tomatoes
  • pesto sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Saute Garlic in pan
  3. Once garlic turns golden add chicken
  4. Drain pasta and then once chicken is cooked through, add chicken to pan
  5. Add pesto sauce and sun-dried tomates and combine and serve
