Classic Waffles

Credit: Allrecipes.com

Description

A recipe to make classic waffles

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, salt, baking soda, sugar
  2. In bowl, beat eggs and stir in milk, butter, vanilla
  3. Ladle the mixture into waffle iron and cook until golden and cooked through
