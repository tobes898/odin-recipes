Oven Baked Omelet

Credit: Allrecipes.com

Description

A baked omelet recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sour Cream
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Green Onions
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Beat eggs, sour cream, milk, and salt in a bowl. Stir in green onions.
  3. Bake in oven for 25-30 min until cooked though and sprikle cheese over top once done
